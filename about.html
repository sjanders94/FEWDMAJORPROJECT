About Us

Anderson's Theatre was built in 1995 to bring year-round movie entertainment to residents of Mount Sterling and neighboring communities. Anderson's Theatre is located in the heart of downtown Mount Sterling We offer 6 movie screen areas that can hold up to 75 people. We frequently add new movies to our location to give our customers options to view new movies out on the market.

Let Anderson's host your next birthday party or family night! A bowling alley and arcade are located inside the same building as the theatre so that a fun, safe time can be accessed in one location!
